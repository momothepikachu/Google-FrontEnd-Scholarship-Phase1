Toby

This is a portrait I drew for Toby, our family pet and best friend. He is Peke-A-Tese, which is a mix between Maltese and Pekingese. Toby's favorite thing to do is sleeping on the couch, lying in the sunny spot or napping on our laps.

  • Scientific Name: Peke-A-Tese
  • Average Length: 10-13 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 10-18 years
  • Personality Traits: Friendly, Playful

Toby is a member of our family. He was very little when we first took him home 12 years ago. Now he is still a small dog, but he is never shy to challenge larger dogs by barking at them fiercely when we walk him outside of the house. Anyways, we love him, and I know that he loves us too.
